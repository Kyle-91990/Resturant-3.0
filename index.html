Our Story

Founded in 2010, Savanna Bites began as a small family-owned eatery in Nairobi's vibrant Westlands district. What started as a humble nyama choma spot has blossomed into one of the city's most beloved restaurants.

We take pride in sourcing the freshest local ingredients and preparing them with traditional techniques passed down through generations, while adding contemporary twists that surprise and delight.
